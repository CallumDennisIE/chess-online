How to Play:

The Basics

Chess Online is a variant of the classic game chess. In this game, 2 players use different pieces to move around the chessboard and try to capture all of their opponents pieces.

Each piece has a different set of moves and a different points cost if they are captured. The player the captures all of their opoents peices (and gets all 52 points) wins!

Where can my Piece Move to?

To move see the availble moves for a specific piece, click on it. The pieces that can be moved too are highlighted with a 'X'.

Please note, that the selected piece much match the same colour, as the current players turn (shown underneath the chessboard), otherwise the movable squares will not be highlighted.

If a piece cannot move, then no highlighted squares will be shown.

To clear the squares marked with an 'X', click anywhere on the chessboard except for a square makred with an 'X'.

If the square is marked with an 'X' and the square is red, then that square is occupied with an opposing piece and can be captured!

How to Move a Piece

To move a piece to a highlighted square, click on the highlighted square.

If your piece is moving to a square that contains an opposing piece, then you will capture that piece and gain a number of points, depending on the pieces value.
